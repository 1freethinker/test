There are 3 ways to get to KoRoot Guesthouse from the airport:

1.Use KAL Limousine Bus #1 to Koreana Hotel (more expensive but convenient)

Step 1. Purchase ticket to Koreana Hotel at KAL limousine counter NO.22
or at designated bus stops on the first floor at Eastern side 4B and Western side 11A.
Look for KAL Bus No.1 Fare is 15,000 Won.

Step 2. Catch a taxi in front of the Koreana Hotel.

Step 3. Show taxi driver the address written in Korean or give our phone number (02-3210- 2451)
or mobile phone number (010-5444- 2451) to get directions to Koroot.
The Koroot address is: 서울특별시 종로구 자하문로 125-10 (청운동 53-56)

2. Take Airport limousine bus #6011 to Gyeongbokgung Station (cheaper than KAL)

Step 1. Take a limousine bus to Gyeongbokgung Station. Purchase tickets at bus stop Eastern side 5B,
or Western side 12A. Fare: 10,000 Won per person

Step 2. Get off at Gyeongbokgung Station and cross the road to Exit No. 3 and take taxi from there.

Step 3. Give taxi driver our phone number or the address to Koroot. It takes less than 10 minutes and costs less than 3,000 won.

3. Take the AREX Airport Train to Seoul Station (all-stop or express)

Step 1. Buy ticket to Seoul station at B4 level.
All stop train is about 50% cheaper than express train
but both go to Seoul Station, which is the last stop.

Step 2. Take a taxi as above or take bus no. 1711, 7016, or 7018.
The buses will make a U-turn to head north to Koroot.

Step 3. You will pass Gyeongbokgung Station after around 10 stops.
3 stops after Gyeongbokgung station get off at Gyeong-gi Commercial High school.

Step 4. Look for the dry cleaning shop on the opposite side of the road.
Walk to the traffic light to cross the road to get to dry cleaning shop (Hyundai dry cleaning).
As soon as you pass the shop, turn right and walk down the alley. Go straight ahead for about 20m,
and KoRoot will appear on your right.

(Notice! If you take a taxi you should leave Seoul station through the back entrance, NOT the main entrance.
You will see a line of cabs waiting right outside the back entrance. The fee should be less than 10,000 won depending on traffic).

About Koroot

KoRoot is a large residential home with a beautiful garden located between Bukhansan Mountain toward the north and the historical Gwanghwamoon district of Seoul toward the south. It is on several bus routes and about a 15 minute walk from Gyeongbukgung subway station. KoRoot Guesthouse has the capacity to host 14 guests daily and is providing around 3000 nights on a yearly basis. 

POLICIES

RESERVATION POLICIES

Reservations will be made on a first-come, first-serve basis. Your reservation will be confirmed by e-mail within two to three working days. If you would like to stay in summer or winter (the busy seasons), we recommend that you make a reservation in advance.

PAYMENT

Payment must be made in full upon check in. However, there is a separate charge for laundering and drying (2,000 KRW for each).

CANCELLATION

If you wish to adjust or cancel your reservation, please give at least one week notice by email.

ARRIVAL

Because of staff availability, please notify us of the approximate time at which you will arrive at KoRoot Guesthouse.

DEPARTURE

All guests must check out promptly at 11AM in order to allow us to make necessary arrangements for the next guests. Please bring your bedsheets and blanket to the laundry room when you check out. If you decide to depart from KoRoot Guesthouse earlier than you were originally scheduled, we expect you to give us two weeks notice.

MAXIMIM DURATION OF STAY

In order to meet the great demand for reservations, the maximum period of stay is three months. If you would like to stay longer, we request you to contact us first.

KoRoot Guesthouse is reserved only for international Korean adoptees. Thank you for your understanding.
