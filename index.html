Welcome to Koroot!

We opened in July 2003 and we welcome more than 300 adoptees annually. 
Adoptees who stay at KoRoot Guesthouse come from a wide variety of backgrounds and nationalities,
with diverse individual stories.KoRoot strives to build global solidarity among overseas adoptees,
working with and for the adoptee community, promoting sharing and exchange knowledge and friendship.
The staff and volunteers are warm and dedicated, and a valuable resource for many adoptees.
We hope to see you soon!
